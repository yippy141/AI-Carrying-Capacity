# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Policy researchers, analysts, funders, journalists, and technical readers who need to understand AI conversion capacity without confusing scaffolding with evidence. They are likely reading during research review, memo drafting, or product exploration.

## Product Purpose

AI Conversion Atlas presents a transparent research framework for studying how societies convert accessible AI capability into material outcomes. Success means readers can see the methodology, source status, missingness, and sector modules without encountering fabricated scores or premature country rankings.

## Brand Personality

Careful, exacting, public-minded. The interface should feel like a rigorous atlas in draft form: calm enough for research, but visually specific enough to be memorable.

## Anti-references

Avoid SaaS dashboards that imply live precision, scorecard templates that collapse dimensions into one number, generic AI-purple gradients, and editorial flourishes that make missing data feel polished enough to trust.

## Design Principles

- Evidence before assertion.
- Missingness is a first-class state.
- Separate analytic layers before comparing countries.
- Let methodology and source status be visible in the interface.
- Treat V0 as a scaffold, not a leaderboard.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use WCAG AA as the baseline assumption. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus states, readable contrast, reduced-motion preferences, and plain-language labels for missing, placeholder, estimated, qualitative-coded, official-claim, and observed evidence states.
