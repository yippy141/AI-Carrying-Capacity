# Paste-Back Feedback For PM Chat

I set up the repo as a scaffold-only control plane rather than starting the product or dataset. That was the right move because the project will live or die on source discipline: it needs a place for Deep Research reports, source extraction, indicator candidates, methodology decisions, and data-quality rules before any UI or score exists.

I agree with the plan's main direction: use "AI carrying capacity" as the public hook, but use "AI conversion capacity" or "national AI absorptive capacity" as the formal construct. The reasoning is that "carrying capacity" implies a fixed ceiling, while this project is about dynamic, sector-specific conversion of capability into outcomes.

The repo now encodes three guardrails I think are important:

1. Do not build a single V0 national score. Keep frontier access, conversion capacity, diffusion speed, adaptation stress, distribution quality, and realized outcomes separate until the data and sensitivity analysis justify aggregation.
2. Treat China-US comparisons as hypotheses, not conclusions. China may have conversion advantages in embodied and industrial AI, while the US may have advantages in frontier models, software, science, cloud, and entrepreneurial diffusion. The project should test where each advantage matters.
3. Keep sources and indicators review-gated. Deep Research outputs should land in staging first, then be extracted into source-register and indicator-candidate files before anything becomes canonical.

I did not initialize Next.js yet. Reason: the research architecture is not locked and the commissioned reports are still pending. Starting with app code would create a premature center of gravity around visuals instead of evidence. Once the first synthesis and indicator catalog are reviewed, the repo is ready to support a product build.

One extra recommendation: add a formal "claim ledger" after the research reports arrive. The project will make many tempting claims about China, US fragmentation, centralization, compute, public tolerance, and industrial diffusion. A claim ledger with fields for claim, evidence type, source IDs, confidence, counterevidence, and product-use status would prevent the launch essay and UI from outrunning the evidence.
